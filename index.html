About this survey

This survey measures your site's safety culture maturity across five key areas: Reporting & Communication, Learning & Improvement, Risk Management & Awareness, Leadership & Trust, and Behaviour & Culture. Results are mapped to the Hudson Safety Culture Ladder, a widely recognised framework used across COMAH and high-hazard industries.

How it works

  • You will be asked 30 statements — rate each one from 1 (Strongly Disagree) to 6 (Strongly Agree)
  • You can add an optional comment to any question
  • The survey typically takes 10–15 minutes to complete
  • Your progress is saved automatically — you can close the page and return later

Anonymity

Your responses are completely anonymous. Your unique access code is used only to link your answers together — it is not connected to your name, role, or any personal information. Please answer honestly to help build an accurate picture of our safety culture.
